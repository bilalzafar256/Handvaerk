## Product Category

**Field Service Management (FSM) + Invoicing SaaS for Danish tradespeople (VVS, electricians, carpenters, painters, masons).**

Two distinct tiers exist in this market:
1. **Craft Management Tier** — Ordrestyring, Apacta, Minuba: order/case → time tracking → quality assurance → invoice
2. **Accounting Tier** — e-conomic, Dinero, Billy: invoicing → bookkeeping → VAT → integrations

The standard market architecture: **craft management software integrates WITH accounting software**. Neither replaces the other. Håndværk Pro currently sits in Tier 1 and must decide whether to integrate with Tier 2 or try to absorb it.

## Critical Market Timing Risk

**⚠️ PEPPOL BIS 4 mandatory in Denmark by May 15, 2026 (1 month away as of writing)**

All invoicing software must support PEPPOL BIS 4 e-invoicing format. Dinero and e-conomic already compliant. Ordrestyring, Apacta, and Minuba status [UNVERIFIED].

Håndværk Pro has `oioubl_format` flag and `peppol_id` in the DB schema but zero frontend/export implementation. **This is a launch blocker for any customer invoicing Danish public sector buyers.**

Sources: peppol.nu, ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks, eurotoolkit.eu

## Top 3 Strategic Insights

1. **Ordrestyring's OS3 is creating a market opening right now.** 30,000+ users, 4.0/5 rating, active complaints about performance and navigation. These users are evaluating alternatives. The window is 12–18 months before Ordrestyring fixes it.

2. **The shared gap across ALL craft software: automated payment reminders, shareable quote links, and AI job recording.** Håndværk Pro has all three already. None of the competitors have any. This is the entire marketing message for launch.

3. **PEPPOL BIS 4 compliance is mandatory by May 15, 2026** and the DB schema is ready — but the export UI isn't built. Every Danish tradesperson who invoices the public sector (hospitals, municipalities, schools) **cannot use Håndværk Pro until this is shipped**. It is both a launch blocker and a competitive opportunity if shipped before competitors confirm compliance.
